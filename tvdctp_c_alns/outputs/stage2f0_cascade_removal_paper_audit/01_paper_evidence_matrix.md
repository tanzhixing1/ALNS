## Definition-level summary

- EXPLICIT: recursive propagation, closure termination, final `R*`, multi-chain union.
- PARTIAL: seed selection, removal quantity, randomness, dependency definition/direction, related routes/sub-routes, bundle partition, duplicate membership.
- UNSPECIFIED: dependency order, tie-breaking, infeasible-expansion handling.

Formula (95) is repair-side evidence for bundle meaning; it does not define a Native removal-side customer ordering.
