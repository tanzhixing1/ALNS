# External-boundary protection

External customers never become bundle members. The adapted scope records the
A.1 boundary customer identities, while the unchanged Cascade scope guard uses
a normalized business projection for every customer outside the active bundle.

The projection protects service modes, unassigned status, outside-customer
route order, unrelated sorties, assignments, stage-1/container structure,
home/carrier maps and transshipment choice. It permits container rewrites only
inside the declared affected scope. Real Ω(B) repair of the selected `{5}` /
actual `{5,7}` fixture preserves the complete normalized outside projection.
